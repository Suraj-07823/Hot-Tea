About Hot Tea

Hot Tea began as a small idea—serve honest, tasty food without compromise on hygiene. We cook fresh, keep the kitchen spotless, and welcome every guest like a neighbour.

Why we started

We wanted a place where people could enjoy quick bites and great tea without worrying about quality or cleanliness. That meant clear processes, trained staff, and consistent recipes.

Our promise

  • Hygienic food preparation
  • Quality ingredients, daily
  • Friendly, local service
